Insurance Help

Insurance Claims: We Do The Paperwork

If your medical insurance covers vision care, or if you are a member of a specialized vision plan, let us help you do the paperwork. Our office staff can help you understand your coverage and can assist in submitting claim forms for prompt and accurate processing. We do not charge for this service.

Talk to us at check-in, before your exam, if you would like us to assist. Or, call the office at 972-527-2020 for answers to any questions you may have. We are here to serve you!
