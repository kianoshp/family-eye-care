Cataracts

Like gray hair and wrinkles, one of the inevitable signs of aging is the formation of cataracts. Cataracts make the lens on your eyes seem cloudy, blurring your vision. However, cataracts don't have to affect your lifestyle. They can be treated. Dr. Garza uses a simple and painless pupil dilation process to diagnose cataracts. From there, he can help you develop the right treatment plan to fit your individual needs.
