Dry Eyes

One the most common reasons people visit an eye doctor is to relieve the burning, dryness or scratchiness of dry eyes, caused by a lack of natural tears. Chronic dry eyes can lead to infections or scarring. If left untreated, more serious problems can occur. Talk to Dr. Garza or his staff if you are experiencing dry, scratchy eyes. Many remedies are simple and can be conducted in the comfort of your own home.
