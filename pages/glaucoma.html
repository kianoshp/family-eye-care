Glaucoma

Most people never know they have glaucoma until it's too late. Like a thief in the night, it can steal your vision if left undetected. That's why it's wise to have your eyes checked regularly by a professional optometrist like Dr. Garza. Instead of using the "puffy air" test no one enjoys, Dr. Garza uses a more effective test called applanation tonometry, a more comfortable and accurate way to test for glaucoma.
