Macular Degeneration

Macular degeneration is a disease that affects your central vision. It is most common among Caucasian females (especially smokers), people age 50 and up, or anyone with a strong family history of the disease. The good news is, if detected early, macular degeneration can be minimized or prevented using remedies as simple as wearing sunglasses or changing your diet.
