Optomap ® Tutorial

An Optomap® Retinal Exam allows Dr. Garza to view almost 80% of your retina, painlessly and easily. Used in addition to or in place of pupil dilation, the technology provides a panoramic view of the back of your eye (the retina), revealing abnormalities that may be compromising your vision. The procedure takes only a few minutes and can help detect many common diseases, including glaucoma, diabetes, macular degeneration, and even cancer.
