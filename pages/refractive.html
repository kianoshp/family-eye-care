Refractive Errors

What’s the difference between near-sighted (myopia) and far-sighted (hyperopia)? What is astigmatism? What is presbyopia? All are refractive errors that cause light to focus on the retina at improper angles, resulting in vision problems. This video explains each condition and its cause in terms anyone can understand.
