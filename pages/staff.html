Tammy C. McDaniel, Optician & General Manager

"I enjoy educating people on the importance of seeing. You only get two eyes. Don't take them for granted!"

Tammy C. McDaniel received her Associate Degree from El Camino College in Gardena, California. She is certified and licensed as an optician with the American Board of Optometry and has spent her entire career in the eye care industry.

Tammy began as an optician for Lens Crafters in California, before advancing to manage a company store in Dallas, Texas. Later, she joined Dallas Eye Care, serving as an optical office and laboratory manager, and took an outside sales position with Marchon, Inc. after that.

Her success in sales soon attracted American Vision Lab, who hired her to run a territory in Texas. When Dr. Garza opened his practice, he asked Tammy to manage his office. Today, she is the front face of Complete Family Eye Care in Plano, Texas.
